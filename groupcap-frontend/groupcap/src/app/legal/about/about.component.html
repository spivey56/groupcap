About GroupCap

GroupCap.co is designed to help groups comfortably and anonymously agree on a budget for trips, events, gifts, or any shared expense. By allowing each person to submit their budget privately, GroupCap removes the social pressure that often leads people to overspend or feel uncomfortable speaking up.

Our purpose is simple: to make group budgeting fair, stress‑free, and transparent so everyone feels respected and included in the final decision.

Why GroupCap Exists

In many group situations, people hesitate to share what they can realistically afford. This can create awkwardness, imbalance, or silent frustration. GroupCap solves this by keeping every budget submission completely anonymous, enabling the group to decide on a comfortable budget without anyone feeling singled out or judged.

Our Focus

  • Privacy: All budget submissions are anonymous.
  • Simplicity: No accounts or complex setup—just quick, clear results.
  • Fairness: Everyone’s comfort level matters equally.
  • Clarity: Results are presented in a straightforward, easy‑to‑understand format.
  • Inclusivity: Designed to help groups make decisions that work for everyone.

Who It's For

GroupCap is great for friends, families, teams, clubs, or any group planning something together—whether it's a trip, outing, shared purchase, or event.

Our Location

GroupCap is operated out of Columbus, Ohio, with ongoing improvements based on real user feedback.
